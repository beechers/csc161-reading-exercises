For the string, 0100001011011001000000000000

The 0 at the beginning represents a positive number.

10000101 forms the binary numeral 133 and represents the exponent
6 when the bias of 127 is removed

Its mantissa is expressed by 1.1011001000000000000 and expresses
the number 217/128 so the number is

2^6 * 217/128 = 108.5
